## 1. Channel overview

Alex Hormozi runs Acquisition.com. His Shorts feed is the single best-distributed example of *talking head + B-roll + bold burned-in text*, optimized to win the first 1.5s.

Recurring visual motifs in the sample:

- **Acquisition.com branded apparel** — same person, same uniform (black/white Acquisition.com tank + cap). Visual identity = HIM.
- **Three setting modes** in rotation: (a) outdoor walking (palm-tree desert street, sunlit), (b) studio room (linear vertical light bars on navy), (c) stage talk (purple-lit slide projection behind). Some have (d) plane interior.
- **B-roll interleave at 3–5s cadence** — same audio plays continuously, video cuts between settings.
- **Burned-in captions in every Short** — never relies on sound being on.
- **Persistent top hook caption** in 6/10 sampled Shorts.

## 4. Cross-template patterns (Hormozi's house grammar)

### Always-present (the structural moat)
1. Karaoke caption track — every Short
2. One-word emphasis per phrase — every Short
3. Continuous audio + visual cuts (audio carries rhythm, video cuts every 3-5s)

### Strongly-present (6+ of 10)
4. Persistent top hook pill
5. B-roll cutting at 3-5s cadence (>=2 settings)
6. Acquisition.com brand uniform

### Periodic (3-5 of 10)
7. Chapter cards (numbered listicle structure)
8. Opening title card (named-theme listicle)

### Distinctive (1-2 of 10, high signal)
9. Emoji icon trio above hook
10. Bracket-label syntax for named options

### Typography system
- **Sans (Helvetica-Now / Inter-style):** bold 700-800 for all hooks, chapter cards, karaoke
- **Italic serif/sans:** subtitles under chapter cards, dialogue-mode karaoke
- **All caps:** yellow-pill (G), yellow-glow (K) — signals louder/aggressive emphasis
- **Curly quotes:** always smart quotes, never straight quotes

### Color discipline (sound-off readability)

| Theme | Bg pill color | Text color | When used |
|---|---|---|---|
| white-pill | `#FFFFFF` @ 85% | `#000000` | Outdoor / bright B-roll |
| yellow-pill | `#FFD500` | `#000000` | High-impact emphasis on dark B-roll |
| yellow-glow | none | `#FFD500` + black shadow | Mid-brightness B-roll, casual feel |
| plain-shadow | none | `#FFFFFF` + thick black shadow | Stage talk with controlled lighting |
