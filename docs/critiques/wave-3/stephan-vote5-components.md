## 🔴/🟠/🟢 Components to Add to Our Library (Top 10)

Prioritized by reuse-score × current absence from our pipeline. 🔴 = must-have, 🟠 = high-value, 🟢 = nice-to-have specialization.

1. 🔴 **`<AppIconBadge>`** — rounded-square tile (~radius 80px) that renders a logo URL/asset on a solid-color BG, with optional sub-label. Drives Stephan's "naming-machine" scenes (L1/B1). Used 3× in one 28s reel. Should accept `bgColor`, `logoUrl`, `label`, `tilt` (degrees). **Reuse: 5**
2. 🔴 **`<MacroLetter>`** — single uppercase character at ~300pt, white, centered, drop-shadow, with hard-snap entrance. Pair with `<Caption>` below for the "B / the most" pattern (C6/T5). **Reuse: 4**
3. 🔴 **`<SplitScreen5050>`** — vertical 50/50 split, top panel takes B-roll/screenshot, bottom panel takes face-cam; caption straddles the seam. This is Stephan's most-used composition (C3, CP6). **Reuse: 5**
4. 🔴 **`<HeroSerifCaption>`** — caption where the first letter of a key word renders in a high-contrast Didone serif italic, rest in standard bold sans. Drives the "**B**lackberry / **V** feels alive" effect (T2, CP3). **Reuse: 3** but signature.
5. 🟠 **`<FloatingAppCard>`** — rounded-corner screenshot card with subtle drop-shadow + slight perspective tilt, composited over a desktop-wallpaper BG. Used for every Claude-app reveal (C5). **Reuse: 5** for our AI-news category.
6. 🟠 **`<FilmStripChrome>`** — vertical sprocket-perforation overlay (UNFOLD 2010TX aesthetic) with sequential frame numbers; sits on top of any black BG (C1). High-impact "cinematic poster" framing. **Reuse: 3**
7. 🟠 **`<CornerBrackets>`** — TR/TL/BL/BR L-shaped focus marks framing a central content card (C2). Used for the "Spent 40 to" framed-quote pattern. **Reuse: 4**
8. 🟠 **`<StephanBlueBG>`** — preset background: royal-blue radial gradient + two thin orbital arc lines + 5% grain. The signature "naming-machine" canvas (BG1). Should be a 1-line preset alongside our existing `#1B3A6E` navy. **Reuse: 4**
9. 🟢 **`<NumberCountUp>`** — odometer-style number reveal inside a corner-bracketed framed card (A6). Adds tension to stat callouts. **Reuse: 3**
10. 🟢 **`<MoviePosterShot>`** — preset wrapper that loads a movie-still B-roll (Godfather/Peaky pattern) with Ken Burns slow zoom + warm color filter unifier. Wraps S3/S4/A3 into one component. **Reuse: 3**

### Cross-cutting observations (not components, but design rules to absorb)

- **Hard-cut everything.** A7/X1: no transitions between scenes. Build our pipeline to treat hard cuts as the default.
- **Monochrome captions, never colored.** CP1/CP7: white + drop-shadow only. Resist the urge to color-code captions.
- **One accent color does the lift.** K1: ~80% of all branded scenes use the same blue. Discipline > variety.
- **Captions adapt position by shot type.** CP5: bottom-third on face-cam, vertical-center on B-roll, top-seam on split-screen. Encode this as a prop, not three components.
- **Real brands as credibility props.** L1: he name-drops Swiffer/Pentium/BlackBerry visually, not just verbally. Our library should support drop-in real-logo references with a `<BrandTile>` API.
