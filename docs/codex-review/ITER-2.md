Overall verdict: the Abhi rendered templates look visually coherent in the contact sheets, and the iter-1 fixes for the counter, transparent text, TypeScript config, and duration-based sampler appear to hold. The remaining serious problems are gallery reliability and a few cross-creator renders that are not exercising the intended pattern: `ABHI-COMPARE.html` points every video at nonexistent slug filenames, the cross-creator source panels still reuse one frame directory per creator instead of the matching source scene, and several media/title-card templates render blank or placeholder-heavy frames during the comparison window.
