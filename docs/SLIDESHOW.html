Strategic finding · @builtbystephan

It's NOT a HeyGen avatar.

Stefan Andrei is on a fixed home-studio cam, not an AI avatar. Confirmed by consistent lavalier-mic, painting, plant, and shelf across 24 keyframes from DYkyJfxx5Lx + 7 other reels in the same room.

The "dynamic crop" pattern you liked is a continuous face-cam take being cropped + cut into by B-roll lanes. Three crop modes:

Crop mode~% runtimeWhat viewer sees
FACE_FULL~30%Stefan fills 1080×1920
BROLL_FULL~25%B-roll asset fills frame; face-cam hidden (audio continues)
SPLIT_50_TOP_BROLL~45% (dominant)B-roll top half; face-cam cropped to bottom half via clipPath: inset(50% 0 0 0)
  • HARD CUTS only — no dissolves, no slides
  • ~22 cuts in 28 s — cadence ~1 cut/sec
  • Every cut lands on a captioned-word onset
  • B-roll lane mixes 5 asset categories per reel: brand vignettes, product cards, movie stills, film-frame chrome, Claude screen-recs
